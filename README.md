# Sup
A vtol that's highly customisible and reliable

i have been into the hobby of flying from planes to drone and really wanted to try and know abt the vtols from a long time and checking out there i didnt really find alot of good customisible options for vtol and in this process ive seen the zipline the company that aims to deliver stuff through drone have made their vtol for urban mobility which really inspired me and now im trying to build on my own which has the vtol capabilites for now as v1 and lets see if this goes thru well i may build that payload model as v2 ;)


The project works on a teensy 4.1 with a gyro9250 as the imu and the dRehmFlight firmware(https://github.com/nickrehm/dRehmFlight.git).
